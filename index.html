His Career

Early Life. Montesquieu was born in the region of Bordeaux, France, on January 18, 1689, to a wealthy family, with maternal connections to the barony. His soldier father also had noble lineage. He went on to take up law at the University of Bordeaux and began working in Paris after graduation.

His Theory

He believed that the power should not be held by one person, it should be seperated into 3 branches, which are Legislative, Executive, and Judicial.

  • Legislative: Responsible about making laws
  • Executive: Responsible about carrying out laws
  • Judicial: Responsible about evaluating laws
